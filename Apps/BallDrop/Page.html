Free Fall

The simulation here demonstrates the free fall motion of a ball. The left hand side shows what the scene looks like to an onlooker: a ball falling straight towards the ground at increasing speed. The right hand side shows you what the respective "position v.s. time" curve would look like if you were to record this data.
Observe how increasing and decreasing air resistance affects the shape of the graph
