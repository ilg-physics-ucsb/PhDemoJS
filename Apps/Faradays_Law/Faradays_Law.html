This applet contains a classic demonstration of Faraday's Law, where an EMF is generated across the voltmeter as you drag the coil into a plane with a uniform magnetic field. The reading on the bottom shows the resulting EMF in real time. You can click and drag the coil, as well as change its area, as well as the value and direction of the magnetic field.
Area: 1 m²

Magnetic Field: 0.1 T
